# Planning Rubric

Score each dimension from 1 (weak) to 5 (strong):

- Clarity: Is the goal, scope, and desired outcome explicit?
- Completeness: Does the plan include files, sub-tasks, acceptance criteria, risks, and validation?
- Testability: Are acceptance criteria measurable and verifiable?
- Risk coverage: Are key unknowns and failure modes identified with mitigation steps?
- Executability: Can a Worker execute the plan without making new decisions?

Interpretation:
- 22-25: Ready for handoff.
- 17-21: Usable with minor gaps; tighten before handoff.
- <=16: Rework required before implementation.
